Meals for Wheel - FAQ

Meals for Wheel is a community initiative that provides nutritious meals to wheelchair users and people with limited mobility.
You can sign up by contacting our support team via the “Contact Us” page or by calling our hotline. Eligibility criteria apply.
Yes, we offer meal options that cater to common dietary restrictions including vegetarian, gluten-free, and diabetic-friendly menus.
Meals for Wheel is a charitable program, so meals are provided free of charge to qualifying individuals.
Meals can be delivered daily or weekly depending on your preference and availability in your area.
